Eric Van Horn

I’m a student at East Stroudsburg University of Pennsylvania, majoring in Digital Media Technologies with a concentration in Graphic & Web Design. While my main focus is on graphic and web design, I’ve had the chance to explore many aspects of media production, including video, photography, sound recording, and more. I consider myself to be proficient in all aspects of media production, with a broad skill set that allows me to tackle a wide range of projects.


Whether I’m designing a website or shooting a video, I’m motivated by bringing an idea to life and making it the best it can be. I don’t believe in settling for "good enough." I’m flexible in my approach and often find that the best ideas come to me as I go, rather than following a rigid plan.


While I’m still building my portfolio, most of the work you’ll see on this site comes from class projects. These projects have allowed me to develop a variety of skills and experiment with different media. I’m always looking for opportunities to learn and grow, and I’m excited to continue expanding my skill set.

Ai In Media

I often incorporate AI tools into my workflow. Whether I’m brainstorming ideas and concepts or I'm stuck on something, AI acts as a sounding board, much like bouncing ideas off another person. If I have a rough concept or need fresh perspectives, I input it into AI and see where it takes me. This helps me visualize ideas quickly, especially for designs or images, allowing me to explore what a concept could look like before dedicating hours to actually creating it. I also use AI to troubleshoot or learn new techniques, such as getting quick tips on how to perform specific tasks or a to get a snippet of code. It helps me push my boundaries and fill in the gaps where I’m still learning or experimenting, making my work more efficient and improved.
